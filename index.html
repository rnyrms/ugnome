UGNOME

UGNOME is an AI-based Dungeon Master for tabletop roleplaying games.

What is UGNOME

In the fantasy role-playing game Dungeons & Dragons, the Dungeon Master (DM for short) is a key player and acts as a combination of referee and storyteller. The DM's job is to create a believable world in which all the players can interact. UGNOME make it easy for you to become your own DM by creating dungeons and monsters using artificial intelligence. It's an AI game master, or dungeon master if you'd like.
